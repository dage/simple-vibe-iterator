**Create an interactive 3D scene using three.js.**

Build a small forest clearing: scatter multiple trees from the **Tree OBJ** around the scene and tile the **Grass Texture** across the ground.  Place a pond in the centre with the **Duck GLB** swimming on it, and nearby add the **BoomBox GLB** (you can attach a sound file so users hear music when they click it).  Position the **Soldier GLB** and **Fox GLB** (see additional resources) walking along a path, and hide a mythical statue made from **Cerberus OBJ** in the shadows.  Hang the **DamagedHelmet GLTF** from a tree branch to add sci‑fi flavour, and stack some crates textured with the **Crate Texture**.  For fun, include a spinning monkey head using the **Suzanne BufferGeometry JSON** and allow users to drag it around.  Wrap the entire scene in a **Bridge2 skybox** for realistic reflections and lighting, and animate the sun so shadows move over time.  Add orbit controls, mouse‑over highlights, and click‑to‑trigger animations to make the experience interactive.  Feel free to extend with particle effects (e.g., fireflies) and post‑processing (bloom, depth‑of‑field) for atmosphere.
